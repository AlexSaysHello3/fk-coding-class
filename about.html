Course Instructors

All of CYCC's teachers are Christ Central members and full-time software engineers.

Andy

I studied computer science at UVA and now work as a full-stack software engineer at bti360. I think everyone should take a coding class at some point in their life. Outside of work I enjoy hanging with my family, watching k-pop, and playing video games.

Vincent

I originally studied systems engineering in college to honor my parents, but after my 2nd semester intro to cs class, I switched to computer science. I've been doing software ever since. By day I write the codes and by night I dream the codes. Just kidding. By night, I'm a husband trying to navigate married/quarantine life. I enjoy reading, writing, playing games, music, and cooking on the side.

Carol

I entered college not even knowing what "coding" was. I took my first intro class as an engineering school requirement, but as soon as my eyes were opened to the virtual world all around us, I wanted to be a part of it. Now I work as a software engineer at Appian and on most days I feel lucky to be doing what I do.

Alexander

Hello, I am Alexander. I was lucky enough to start learning CS in 9th grade because it was required for me to graduate. I think the most important thing is exposure to see if you like it or not. I am looking forward to this camp!
